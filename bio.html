About Me

I’m a rising senior at NYU’s College of Arts and Science, pursuing a joint major in Computer Science and Data Science. I’m well-versed in Python, Java, and C. I have also worked with certain useful libraries within these languages for data science (NumPy, Pandas, PyTorch), data processing/analysis (Hadoop, Hive), and parallel computing (MPI, OpenMP, CUDA). I am also an America Reads/Counts tutor, assisting students and teachers at public schools at no cost to the schools themselves (although I am currently not working because it is summer).

I'm interested in researching misinformation, specifically how data science can be leveraged to identify and analyze it. For example, here is a project in which I compared COVID-19 misinformation themes between two different social media platforms using big data tools.
